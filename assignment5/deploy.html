Deploy your App!

Meteor makes it really easy to put your app on the internet where it can be accessed by the public. To deploy your app, open a Command Prompt window and change the directory to your Meteor project directory. Then, type:

meteor deploy myapp.meteor.com

Just like when we were creating a project, "myapp" represents the name of your project. You should replace all references to "myapp" with the name of your project. After Meteor finishes compiling and uploading your project, you can go to http://myapp.meteor.com to check out your new app! Go ahead and show off your work to your friends!
